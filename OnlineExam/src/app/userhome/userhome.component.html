Let’s Hack!


After a brief pandemic detour, the 5th edition of the BreakingCode is back on track!
We look forward to welcoming developers from across the country to BreakingCode to hack and have fun by solving some real-world problems.

Rules To Remember


- Each question in the quiz is of multiple-choice(MCQ) format.
- No Negative Marking is there.
- After responding to a question, click on the "Next Question" button at the bottom to go to the next questino.
- After responding to the 10th question, click on "Submit" at the bottom of the window to see your score in the quiz.
- The total score for the quiz is based on your responses to all questions.
